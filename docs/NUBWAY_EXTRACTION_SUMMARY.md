## Key Competitive Insights

### NUBWAY's Approach
- **B2B Focus:** Exclusively targets professional medical/aesthetic clinics
- **Quote-Based Pricing:** No transparent pricing, consultation required
- **Technical Emphasis:** Strong R&D and manufacturing credentials
- **Comprehensive Portfolio:** Wide range of professional-grade equipment

### Differentiation Opportunities for Davidov
1. **Hybrid Market Approach:** Serve both professionals and educated consumers
2. **Transparent Pricing:** Clear pricing vs. quote-only competitors
3. **Personalized Service:** Boutique approach vs. large distributor model
4. **Consumer Education:** Comprehensive treatment guidance and education
5. **Local Expertise:** Immediate availability and personalized consultation

## Technical Specifications to Integrate

### Laser Technologies
- **Diode Laser:** 808nm wavelength with cooling technology
- **CO2 Laser:** 10,600nm fractional technology for skin resurfacing
- **Picosecond Laser:** 532nm/1064nm for pigment and tattoo removal
- **Nd:YAG Laser:** 1064nm for vascular and deep treatments

### Body Contouring
- **Cryolipolysis:** Controlled fat cooling for 20-25% reduction
- **EMS Technology:** Electromagnetic muscle stimulation equivalent to 20,000 crunches
- **RF Technology:** Radiofrequency for skin tightening and collagen stimulation

### Skin Treatments
- **HIFU:** High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound for non-surgical lifting
- **IPL:** Broad spectrum light for photorejuvenation
- **HydraFacial:** Multi-step cleansing and hydration technology
